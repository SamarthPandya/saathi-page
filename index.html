"The more you are aware of self. The happier you will be "
We frequently lose ourselves in the race in our competitive world, which makes life duller and unsatisfying. So One should maintain good mental hygiene if they want to be happy. In order to monitor the campus junta 's mental health and well-being throughout the year, the Saathi Club, an IITG mental health support group, conducts a number of activities. Saathi collaborates with the Center for Holistic Well-Being IITG, where counselors offer a safe space, confidentiality, and all the help necessary for the person if something is bothering them. This helps them to have a Smooth Journey while at IITG.
